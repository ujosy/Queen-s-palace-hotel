Food Service / Food Runner

Our dedicated food service team ensures that your dining experience is both enjoyable and seamless. With a menu crafted by top chefs, we bring freshly prepared meals directly to your table or room, always with prompt service and a smile. Whether you're enjoying a meal in our restaurant or prefer in-room dining, our food runners are here to deliver culinary delights at any hour of the day.

Refreshment

Unwind and rejuvenate with our exclusive refreshment services. Take a dip in our crystal-clear swimming pool, relax in the hot tub, or sip on a refreshing drink from our poolside bar. Our pool area provides a serene atmosphere to relax, with comfortable loungers, towels, and a menu of light snacks and beverages to enjoy throughout the day.

Housekeeping

Our housekeeping team is committed to maintaining a clean and comfortable environment for all guests. Each day, we carefully clean and sanitize every room, replenish essentials, and ensure all areas of your room are fresh and welcoming. Special requests, such as additional towels or linens, are just a call away — our team is here to make your stay effortless and enjoyable.

Room Security

Your safety and peace of mind are our priorities. Our security team ensures a safe environment within the hotel premises, with advanced room locking systems, surveillance, and on-call security staff. Every room is equipped with secure access systems, and we offer additional safety services, such as safe deposit boxes for valuables, to give you complete confidence during your stay.
